Self Defence

Heel Palm Strike

The palm heel strike is one of the safest, simplest, and most effective moves for self-defense. To execute it properly without breaking your wrist, curl your fingers back and strike forcefully with the dense, lower part of your palm. For step-by-step form tips, check out Self Defense Basics: Palm Heel Strike by Power Your Safety

How to Execute this Strike

  • Prepare your hands: Put your hands up defensively (as if saying, "I don't want any trouble"), keep your fingers curled back, and protect your head.
  • Shift and rotate: Drive from the ground, rotate your hips, and step slightly off-center to align your shoulder directly behind your hand.
  • Strike: Drive the hard heel of your palm straight into your target.
