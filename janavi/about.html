About Women Safety

Women safety involves protecting women from physical, emotional, and sexual violence through legal, social, and technological interventions to ensure freedom from fear. It is a critical, multi-pronged issue focused on preventing crimes, promoting gender equality, enhancing financial autonomy, and providing safe environments at home, work, and in public spaces

Key Intiatives

  • Legal Protections (India Focus): The Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita 2023 mandates strict penalties, including the death penalty for severe sexual offenses against children.
  • Emergency Infrastructure: Key resources include the 112 Emergency Response Support System (ERSS), 181 Women Helpline, and One Stop Centres (OSCs) for immediate assistance.
  • Workplace Security: The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 makes it mandatory to have an Internal Committee (ICC) in organizations to handle complaints.
  • Technological Interventions: Development of mobile apps for SOS alerts, GPS tracking, and increased CCTV surveillance in public areas
