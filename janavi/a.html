Ready Stance in Self Defense

The ready stance in self-defense is a posture that allows a person to be prepared and ready to take action in a situation where a threat exists. It is a stance that can be adjusted based on the specific circumstances and the nature of the threat. Here are some key points about the ready stance in self-defense

  • Positioning: The ready stance involves positioning the body to be ready to shoot or defend against an attacker. This can include keeping the gun pointed in a safe direction and maintaining a clear field of view.
  • Safety First: It is crucial to prioritize safety above all else when using a ready stance. The gun should not be pointed at anything that could cause harm.
  • Variability: There is no single optimal ready stance for all situations. The ready stance must be situationally dependent and tailored to the specific context of the threat.
  • Awareness: Awareness of the environment and the threat is essential. The ready stance should allow for quick scanning and reaction to potential dangers.
