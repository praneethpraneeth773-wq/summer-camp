About Travel Safely

Travel safety is important for women whether travelling alone or with others. Before starting a journey, women should plan their route, keep important phone numbers saved, and share travel details with trusted family members or friends. Choosing safe transportation, staying in trusted accommodations, and avoiding isolated areas can help reduce risks during travel.

Women should also keep their belongings secure, stay alert in crowded places, and avoid sharing too much personal information with strangers. Carrying emergency items such as a power bank, flashlight, whistle, or small first-aid kit can be helpful during emergencies. Confidence, awareness, and preparation help women travel more safely and comfortably
