What is an Animal Care Center?

Animal care

An Animal Care Center is a place where injured, sick, homeless, and abandoned animals are rescued and protected.

These centers provide food, shelter, medical treatment, and safety for animals. Animal care centers also create awareness about kindness toward animals.

Medical Care for Animals

Doctors and volunteers provide treatment, vaccination, and food for animals.

Animal care centers save thousands of innocent animals every year.

Services of Animal Care Center

Rescue Services

Our team rescues animals from dangerous situations and provides emergency help.

Helping animals is our responsibility.

Wildlife Protection

We protect wild animals like lions, tigers, elephants, and birds from danger.
