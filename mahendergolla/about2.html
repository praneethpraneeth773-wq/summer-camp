About Animal Care Center

We rescue injured and homeless animals and provide them with safety and shelter. Animal care centers help animals live healthy and safe lives.

Main Objectives of Animal Care Centers

1. Animal Rescue

Rescuing animals from roads, forests, accidents, and dangerous situations.

2. Medical Treatment

Providing medicines, surgeries, vaccinations, and healthcare services.

3. Shelter and Food

Giving safe homes, clean water, and healthy food for animals.

4. Wildlife Protection

Protecting wild animals like lions, tigers, elephants, deer, and birds.

5. Adoption Programs

Helping homeless pets find loving families and safe homes.

Importance of Animal Care Centers

Animal care centers help reduce animal cruelty and save many innocent lives. They also help maintain environmental balance and protect wildlife. These centers teach people to love and care for animals.

Animals Protected in Care Centers

Dogs, Cats, Lions, Tigers, Elephants, Birds, Rabbits, Cows, Horses, Monkeys, Deer, and many other animals are protected in animal care centers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe and happy world for animals. We believe every animal deserves love, freedom, food, and protection.
